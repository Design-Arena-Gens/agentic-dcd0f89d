Frequently asked questions

How is FuseNotes different from taking notes in Google Docs? ‚ñº

Google Docs is a blank page. FuseNotes automatically structures your notes into summaries, action items, and searchable records. No more hunting through 50 docs to find "what did we decide about that feature?"

Is my data really private? ‚ñº

Yes. Your notes are encrypted both at rest and in transit using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We can't read your data, and we never train AI models on your content. You can export or delete your data anytime.

Can I integrate with tools like Notion, Slack, or Asana? ‚ñº

Pro and Team plans include one-click export to Notion, Asana, Trello, Linear, and more. We also offer a Slack bot for instant summaries in channels (Team plan only).

What happens after my free trial ends? ‚ñº

You'll be downgraded to the free Starter plan automatically. You keep access to your last 7 days of notes and can upgrade anytime. No credit card required for the trial.

Do you offer refunds? ‚ñº

Yes. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll refund you in full, no questions asked.

How does the action item extraction work? ‚ñº

FuseNotes uses natural language processing to identify tasks, owners, and deadlines from your notes. You can edit or remove any extracted item before finalizing. It learns from your patterns over time.
